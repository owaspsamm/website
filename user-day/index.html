User Day

June 2024, Lisbon

The OWASP SAMM team is thrilled to announce its upcoming User Day, as part of Global AppSec Lisbon , on Wednesday, June 26th.

We invite you to explore our agenda, featuring talks and discussions led by industry experts on topics ranging from the practical applications of SAMM to real-world case studies showcasing its impact. Whether you’re a seasoned practitioner or new to the field, there’s something for everyone to learn and contribute. Register now to secure your spot and join the conversation!

About us

This is an OWASP Project.
OWASP is an open community dedicated to enabling organizations to conceive, develop, acquire, operate, and maintain applications that can be trusted. All of the OWASP tools, documents, forums, and chapters are free and open to anyone interested in improving application security.

OWASP SAMM is published under the CC BY-SA 4.0 license and we share the OWASP Privacy Policy

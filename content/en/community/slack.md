Our [Slack channel](https://owasp.slack.com/messages/C0VF1EJGH) is the quickest way to connect with the SAMM community. Core team members are active and typically respond within a day or two.

## How to join

- If you already have an OWASP Slack account, join the [#samm channel](https://owasp.slack.com/messages/C0VF1EJGH) directly
- If you're new to OWASP Slack, [request an invitation](https://owasp.org/slack/invite) first

## What happens there

- Questions about the model, assessments, and implementation
- Announcements about releases, events, and community calls
- Discussions between practitioners sharing their experiences
- Feedback on ideas, proposals, and contributions

## Other ways to connect

For proposals and threaded conversations, use [GitHub Discussions](/community/github-discussions/). To join live conversations with the core team and practitioners, check out our [Community Calls](/community/community-calls/).

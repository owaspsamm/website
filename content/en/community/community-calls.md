We host community calls on the second Wednesday of every month. Calls may cover project updates, conversations with experts on SAMM-related topics, or open Q&A.

## How to join

Subscribe to our [newsletter](/community/newsletter/) to receive the call link and updates. You can also find event details on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/owasp-samm/), though we plan to move away from Meetup in the near future.

## Recordings

We record project updates and expert conversations and publish them on our [YouTube channel](/community/youtube/). Q&A and open discussions stay off the record. Check out the [SAMM Podcast](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLL2wt4pQkkuKKVlpNKS3775rrFtVPvOoV) playlist for expert conversations.

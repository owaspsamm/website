**Barcelona · April 2026**

Session write-ups from the core team summit. All are consolidated from the session's Notion AI summary and the in-room scratch notes.

## Sessions

- [**CRA & Formalization**](./cra-and-formalization/) — SAMM's implicit persona vs. the Cyber Resilience Act; software-vs-application language; glossary and persona layer. Guidance-first, then contrast with the model to surface what v3 needs.
- [**Stream Guidance**](./stream-guidance/) — quality and structure of stream/activity guidance; merge community + supporting guidance; migrate Google Docs → Markdown → HTML; assessment guidance with personas; case studies; Pat (technical) + Aram (content) as owners.
- [**AI Alignment**](./ai-alignment/) — SAMM in a world of AI-generated code; guidance first, then a per-activity walk-through; package SAMM as a skill an agent can load; *"SAMM is about the process, AI is about the product."*
- [**Operating Cadence & Ownership**](./operating-cadence/) — the recurring work (newsletter, podcast, community calls, sponsors, budgeting, training, events) needs named owners and honest cadences; be smarter, not busier; website launch gated on a communication package; *"if it's owned by everyone, it's owned by nobody."*

Guiding quote from the room: *"The maturity model is the north star. Guidance is how to use it."*

Turn your target into a phased roadmap with timelines and priorities.

![Step 4: Define the Plan](/img/docs/samm-steps-define-the-plan.svg)

## Activities

**Determine change schedule**  
Choose a realistic change strategy in terms of number and duration of phases. A typical roadmap consists of 4 to 6 phases lasting 3 to 12 months each.

**Develop or update the roadmap plan**  
Distribute the implementation of additional activities over the different roadmap phases, taking into account the effort required to implement them. Try to balance the implementation effort across periods and respect dependencies between activities.

## Best practices

- Identify activities that can be completed quickly and successfully early in the project.
- Start with awareness and training.
- Adapt the plan to upcoming release cycles and key projects.
